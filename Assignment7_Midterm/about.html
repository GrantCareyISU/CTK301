About This Site

What is her job? Karen is an Uber driver hired to drive only McDonald's employees. After her outburst at the CEO of McDonald's, she was sued and told that the only way she could avoid being punished was if she put away her pride and drove the people she previously belittled. Instead of taking out her anger on fast food workers, she now screeches at other drivers for stopping at the stop sign. In fact, she decided to ram into the back of one vehicle for not blowing past a red stoplight. Unfortunately for her, the vehicle she hit was a cop car. After she is done with her duties at McDonald's, Karen will be facing criminal charges for her crimes against the human race. The verdict is almost certainly going to be the death penalty.

TABLES! Once Karen has finished her sentence as a driver, she is going to move on to cleaning the dirty tables in various McDonald's restaurants. The CEO has secretly told all the managers to intentionally make all the tables as dirty as possible before her shift. It is most likely that Karen will have to clean bubblegum, maple syrup, and old nuggets caked in grease from the bottoms of the tables. That job will most likely lead to her beating a customer to death, which will only add to her criminal charges.

According to the recent news article, Karen's court verdict has been guilty, and she is to receive the only punishment worse than the death penalty: listening to the "I Play Pokemon Go Everyday" song on loop for the rest of her life.
