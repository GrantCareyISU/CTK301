CTK Requirements

Admission and Retention Requirements:

All students must undergo a portfolio review and/or an interview with the Creative Technologies Admissions Committe before being admitted into the program. The Committee will evaluate admission requests from new beginning freshman, new transfer students, and internal transfers. The Creative Technologies program reserves the right to maintain a balanced enrollment and the number of students wishing to enter the Creative Technologies program may be restricted.

Students will be allowed to continue in the Creative Technologies program who have met and continue to maintain a cumulative GPA of 2.50 and have demonstrated competency through an intermediate portfolio review.

Academic Requirements:

The 55 hours requires for the degree are programmed as follows:

Creative Technologies Core (8 hours)

This group of courses provides the student with a foundation of study in digital media and multimedia design:

  • CTK 201
  • CTK 301
  • CTK 302

Fine Arts Core (11 hours)

This group of courses provides the student with a basic experience in reading and performing music, visual arts experience in drawing and design, and interdisciplinary fine arts history study:

  • 3 hours from the following:
    • ART 103, ART 104, or ART 109
  • and 2 hours from the following:
    • MUS 118 or
    • MUS 121.10 or MUS 126
  • and 6 hours (in two different Schools) from the following:
    • ART 155, ART 156, MUS 152, THE 170, or THE 171.

Areas of Emphasis (18 hours)

This group of courses provides the student with fine arts training in the historical and theoretical foundations, skills, and performance and production experiences of the arts. Choose one of the Fine Arts emphasis from the schools of Art, Music, or Theatre and Dance.

Art Emphasis

6 hours in addition to the Art course completed for the Fine Arts Core from the following courses:

  • ART 103, ART 104, or ART 109

and 12 hours from

  • ART 226, ART 232, ART 233, ART 235, ART 236, ART 252, ART 261, ART 262, ART 284, ART 357, or ART 363.

Music Emphasis

6 hours in addition to the Music course completed for the Fine Arts Core from the following courses:

  • MUS 118 or MUS 119, MUS 121, MUS 126, or from Applied Music study (MUS 131-138 or MUS 231-238)

And 6 hours from the following:

  • MUS 101 and MUS 107, or MUS 102 and MUS 108

And 6 hours from the following:

  • MUS 167, MUS 175, MUS 205, MUS 207, MUS 265, MUS 360, or up to two credit hours of a music ensemble (MUS 139 or MUS 239)

Auditions may be required by the School of Music for some of these courses.

Theatre Emphasis

18 hours from the following:

  • DAN 119
  • THE 101
  • THE 102
  • THE 110
  • THE 130
  • THE 160
  • THE 258
  • THE 260
  • THE 261 or THE 344

Additional Creative Technologies Study (15 hours)

Select from the following courses after completion of the Fine Arts Core, the Creative Technologies Core, and the Creative Technologies intermediate portfolio review.

3 hours from the following:

  • CTK 240, CTK 300, CTK 340, CTK 345, or CTK 380

3 hours from the following:

  • CTK 300, ART 371, THE 360 with graphic topics, or CTK 380

9 hours from any of the following courses, or other courses that emphasize Creative Technologies applications with approval of the program advisor:

  • CTK 260, CTK 300, CTK 320, CTK 340, CTK 345, CTK 380, CTK 390
  • ART 371 or THE 360
  • ART 253, ART 351.24, or THE 367

(Multiple enrollments are allowed in CTK 300 and CTK 380 may be repeated with different topics or experiences).

Capstone Experience in Creative Technologies (3 hours)

Internship in Creative Technologies (CTK 398 or comparable culminating experience in multimedia design as approved by the program advisor e.g. CTK 260)

Assignment Three for CTK 301
