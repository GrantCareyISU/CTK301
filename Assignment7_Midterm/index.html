Dan and the Creeks

Dan Flashes is a man who stil believes he is a teenager who is only popular because he sell illegal substances. He lives on his ex-girlfriend's couch and dresses like a guy from the 80s who was obsessed with Pac-Man. After he got complaints about being too loud while bullying kids in Xbox calls during Call of Duty games, he decided to get a job as a mall cop. He quickly lost his job upon helping a burglar find the diamond store. He is losing his hair faster than most people his age and quite frankly isn't even aware of it. His diet consists only of sweet-potato fries and Sprite. On that note, he has an irrational hatred of Sierra Mist. If he sees anyone drinking a Sierra Mist he will take their bottle and throw it as far as he possible can... which isn't very far.

Shops at the Creeks is an outdoor mall where Dan likes to spend his inheritance money he got from when his parents died. He is usually found in the restrooms due to having finally been kicked out of each and every store because of his behavior.The only reason they allow him on the property is because the burglar he assisted at his former job was actually the owner of the land the outdoor mall is on. Prior to getting banned from going inside, his favorite store at the Shops at the Creeks was the Build-A-Bear workshop. His most chaotic moment at the workshop was when he made two bears and decided they were assassins who intimidated their targets by tap dancing. He imagined the first target of the tap dancing assassins was the employee working at the register at the time. She resigned the day after.

According to the recent news article, Dan Flashes has been arrested. He is currently spending his time in the local insane asylum, where he spends his days kissing spoons while talking about how his cousin will marry him someday.
